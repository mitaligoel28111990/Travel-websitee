KITES(Katha Information Technology & Entrepreneurship School)

Project - Travel

About

The KITES project, "Travel" is designed to enhance children's understanding of science, mathematics, and geography through interactive exploration. This initiative encourages students to analyze different travel routes, calculate distances, and understand time zones while integrating scientific concepts like weather patterns and ecosystems. By engaging in hands-on activities such as map reading, budgeting for trips, and studying cultural landmarks, children develop practical skills in measurement, data interpretation, and global awareness. Through worksheets and practical tasks, they apply these concepts in real-world scenarios, reinforcing their learning. The project fosters curiosity and critical thinking, making education both engaging and meaningful.
